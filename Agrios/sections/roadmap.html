Roadmap

From empty vault to farm intelligence

A staged build — knowledge first, hardware second, intelligence and automation layered on top.

Phase 1 · Weeks 1–8

Build the knowledge base

Stand up the Obsidian vault and run the looping research swarm — broad then deep across every beef-farming domain.

Phase 2 · Parallel

Deploy the farm network

Starlink, 4–5 solar LoRa mesh gateways, weather station, EID tags and the grid soil-sampling programme.

Phase 3

Wire the interaction layer

Telegram bot + Whisper voice, vault Q&A, calendar automation and vet/supplier email actions.

Phase 4

Add machines & lab feeds

Weigh/medicine crate, silage & hay analysis and invoice capture flowing into the vault.

Phase 5 · Ongoing

Intelligence & optimisation

Run analytics for profit and cost; keep the research swarm topped up; close the loop with bespoke recommendations.

Cumulative capability

How the system's usefulness compounds as each phase lands.
