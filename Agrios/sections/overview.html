The Concept

One vault. Every signal. Real intelligence.

Gupworthy Farm pulls in as much information as the farm can produce, stores it as structured knowledge inside an Obsidian vault, then layers reasoning on top so anyone can simply ask and get a grounded answer.

Every sensor reading, animal record and research finding flows into a single structured Obsidian vault — the living memory the whole system reasons from. Hover any node to explore connections. Click a node or row to expand what it stores.

Capture everything

Sensors, machines, documents and human observations all funnel into a single, structured, searchable vault — nothing falls through the cracks.

Reason on top

An intelligence overlay connects research with live farm data, so questions get answers that are specific to this herd, soil and season.

Act automatically

Answers become actions — calendar prompts, vet emails, nutrient payloads and cost alerts — closing the loop from signal to outcome.
