Market data sources — what feeds the vault

Every source below is ingested automatically or semi-automatically into the Obsidian vault as structured, timestamped records.

Sale timing optimizer

The vault models projected deadweight value at 7, 14 and 21 days out against current price trend, flagging the optimal kill date for each pen of finishers.

Bull ROI calculator

Purchase price ÷ (expected EBV gain per calf × current calf price × calves per season) = breakeven. Runs automatically against each bull sale catalogue.

Price alert triggers

Set floor prices for each grade and weight range. The Telegram bot fires an alert the moment the AHDB feed crosses the threshold in either direction.

Kill sheet parsing

Processor PDF kill sheets are forwarded to a dedicated inbox, parsed and each animal's kill-out %, classification and price are logged against its EID record.

Auction catalogue indexing

After each breed society or auction, the LLM reads the online results, extracts EBVs and hammer prices, and updates the bull database with regression-line benchmarks.

Enterprise margin live view

At any time the vault can answer "what is my gross margin per head right now?" — combining live input costs, current market prices and the animal's performance record.
