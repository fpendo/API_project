## The Business Problem (Market Research Context)

### The $3 Trillion Blind Spot

European ETF issuers manage **$3.1 trillion in assets**, yet they have **zero visibility** into who actually owns their products. Here's why:

**The Custody Chain Problem:**
1. When investors buy ETFs through platforms (Hargreaves Lansdown, Interactive Investor) or wealth managers, the shares are held in **nominee accounts**
2. The issuer's share register shows names like:
   - "CREST DEPOSITORY NOMINEES"
   - "EUROCLEAR BANK NOMINEES"
   - "STATE STREET BANK CLIENTS ACCOUNT"
3. These are **intermediaries**, not the actual investors
4. The custody chain can be **3-5 layers deep**: ETF → CSD → Global Custodian → Platform → Wealth Manager → End Client
5. **Issuers have NO IDEA who their real clients are**

### Why This Matters to Capital Markets Teams

**Business Impact:**
- **Lost revenue** - Can't identify high-value institutional relationships for cross-selling
- **Blind distribution** - Don't know which platforms/wealth managers are driving growth
- **Regulatory risk** - Can't comply with beneficial ownership reporting requirements
- **Strategic blindness** - Can't segment customers or target marketing
- **Competitive disadvantage** - Competitors with better data make smarter decisions

**Real Pain Points for Head of Capital Markets:**
- "We have £5B in AUM but no clue if it's retail, institutional, or who our biggest distributors are"
- "We can't prove to the board which distribution partnerships are actually delivering"
- "Our competitors know their ownership profile. We're flying blind."
- "We spend £200K/year on market intelligence but it doesn't tell us about OUR funds"
- "We're considering launching a new product but don't know who would buy it"

## The ETAnalytics Solution

### What We Do

**ETAnalytics is the ONLY platform** that enables ETF issuers to:
1. **Upload their share register** (the CSV they get quarterly)
2. **Automatically match** nominee names against a database of 500+ known entities
3. **Request disclosure** from custodians using Irish Companies Act powers (ETFs domiciled in Ireland have statutory rights to request beneficial ownership data)
4. **Trace the custody chain** through multiple layers to identify the end investment decision maker
5. **Deliver actionable reports** showing WHO owns the ETF, HOW MUCH they hold, and THROUGH WHICH CHANNELS

### The Unique Legal Advantage

**78% of European ETFs are domiciled in Ireland** because of favorable tax and regulatory treatment. The Irish Companies Act grants issuers **statutory disclosure powers** - they can legally compel custodians to reveal beneficial ownership.

**ETAnalytics operationalizes this right.** We handle the entire workflow:
- Generate disclosure requests with proper legal language
- Track responses from custodians
- Reconcile multi-layer custody chains
- Deliver clean, actionable ownership data

**Key messaging:** Don't mention specific section numbers (like "Section 1062"). Keep it ambiguous like "Irish Companies Act disclosure powers" or "statutory disclosure rights" so clients don't think they can easily DIY this.
