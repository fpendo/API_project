# ETAnalytics Logo Design - Quick Brief

## What We Do
**B2B SaaS platform** that helps European ETF issuers identify beneficial owners by unwinding complex custody chains. We turn 5-15% ownership visibility into 60-93% in 90 days.

## Target Audience
Senior executives at ETF issuers (BlackRock, Amundi, Vanguard) - ages 35-55, based in UK/Ireland/Luxembourg/Germany.

---

## Design Direction

### Brand Personality
**5 Words:** Transparent, Intelligent, Trustworthy, Precise, Innovative

### Visual Themes to Explore
1. **Visibility/Insight** - Eye, lens, light revealing hidden layers
2. **Network/Connection** - Nodes, custody chains, ownership trees
3. **Data/Analytics** - Charts, geometric precision, data viz aesthetic
4. **Typography** - Strong modern wordmark with analytical edge

### Style
- **✅ Do:** Modern, clean, minimalist, financial-credible, European sophistication
- **❌ Avoid:** Silicon Valley startup, trendy gradients, corporate-boring, clipart

---

## Colors

### Option 1: Trust & Intelligence (Recommended)
- Primary: Deep Navy `#0F2847` or Financial Blue `#1E3A5F`
- Accent: Bright Teal `#00D9C0` or Electric Blue `#00A8E8`
- Neutral: Charcoal `#2D3748` / White

### Option 2: European Sophistication
- Primary: Oxford Blue `#002147`
- Accent: Gold/Amber `#F59E0B`
- Neutral: Slate Gray `#475569` / Off-White

### Option 3: Modern FinTech
- Primary: Deep Purple `#5B21B6`
- Accent: Cyan `#06B6D4`
- Neutral: Dark Gray `#1F2937` / White

---

## Typography
**Recommended:** Modern geometric sans (Poppins, Montserrat, Inter, Space Grotesk)  
**Avoid:** Serif (too traditional), Script (not serious), Condensed (hard to read)
