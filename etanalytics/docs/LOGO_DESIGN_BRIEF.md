# ETAnalytics Logo Design Brief

**Project:** ETAnalytics Brand Identity  
**Date:** January 2026  
**Version:** 1.0

---

## 1. Company Overview

**Company Name:** ETAnalytics (or ET Analytics)

**Tagline:** "Know Your Investors. Grow Your Assets."

**What We Do:**  
ETAnalytics is a B2B SaaS platform that helps European ETF issuers identify their beneficial owners through automated shareholder register analysis. We unwind complex custody chains to reveal the actual investment decision makers behind nominee accounts—turning 5-15% visibility into 60-93% in 90 days.

**Industry:** Financial Technology (FinTech) / RegTech / Investor Relations Software

**Target Market:** European UCITS ETF issuers (BlackRock, Amundi, Vanguard, etc.)

**Company Stage:** Pre-launch / Seed stage

## 4. Logo Requirements

### Style Direction

**Preferred Style:** Modern, clean, minimalist with a financial/analytical edge

**Visual Themes to Explore:**

1. **Visibility/Revelation**
   - Eye symbol (insight, seeing what's hidden)
   - Lens/magnifying glass (zooming in to find truth)
   - Light breaking through darkness
   - Layers peeling back (revealing what's beneath)

2. **Network/Connection**
   - Nodes and connections (custody chain unwinding)
   - Tree structure (ownership tree)
   - Interconnected dots/lines
   - Network graph visualization

3. **Data/Analytics**
   - Chart/graph elements
   - Data visualization aesthetic
   - Geometric precision
   - Grid patterns

4. **Typography-Based**
   - Strong, modern wordmark
   - "ET" monogram with analytical edge
   - Letterforms that suggest transparency/layers

### Symbol Concepts to Consider

| Symbol | Meaning | Notes |
|--------|---------|-------|
| **Eye** | Insight, visibility, seeing through | Simple, recognizable, suggests "analytics" |
| **Layers/Strata** | Unwinding custody chains | Could be abstract geometric layers |
| **Network nodes** | Connected entities | Modern, tech-forward |
| **Magnifying lens** | Discovery, investigation | Classic but may feel generic |
| **Data points** | Analytics, precision | Could be too abstract |
| **Compass/Navigator** | Finding the path to truth | More metaphorical |

## 5. Color Palette

### Primary Colors

**Option A: Trust & Intelligence**
- **Primary**: Deep Navy Blue `#0F2847` or Financial Blue `#1E3A5F`
- **Secondary**: Bright Accent Teal `#00D9C0` or Electric Blue `#00A8E8`
- **Neutral**: Charcoal `#2D3748` / Clean White `#FFFFFF`

**Option B: European Sophistication**  
- **Primary**: Oxford Blue `#002147` or Prussian Blue `#003153`
- **Secondary**: Gold/Amber accent `#F59E0B` (suggests value, premium)
- **Neutral**: Slate Gray `#475569` / Off-White `#F8FAFC`

**Option C: Modern FinTech**
- **Primary**: Deep Purple `#5B21B6` (innovation, intelligence)
- **Secondary**: Cyan `#06B6D4` (clarity, transparency)
- **Neutral**: Dark Gray `#1F2937` / White

### Color Psychology
- **Blue**: Trust, stability, finance, intelligence (most common in finance)
- **Teal/Cyan**: Clarity, transparency, innovation
- **Purple**: Premium, intelligence, innovation
- **Gray**: Professionalism, neutrality, sophistication
- **Avoid**: Red (too aggressive), Green (too environmental), Orange (too casual)

## 6. Typography Guidance

### Primary Typeface Suggestions

**For Wordmark:**
- **Modern Geometric Sans**: Poppins, Montserrat, Inter, Proxima Nova
- **Financial-grade Sans**: Roboto, Open Sans, Lato
- **Distinctive Sans**: Space Grotesk, Cabinet Grotesk, Aktiv Grotesk

**For "ET" Monogram:**
- **Bold, geometric** letterforms
- Clean, precise, mathematical feel
- Good readability at small sizes

**What to Avoid:**
- ❌ Serif fonts (too traditional/old finance)
- ❌ Script/handwritten fonts (not serious enough)
- ❌ Overly condensed fonts (hard to read)
- ❌ Trendy display fonts (won't age well)

## 7. Logo Variations Needed

### Deliverables

| Type | Usage | Size |
|------|-------|------|
| **Primary Logo** | Full color on light background | Horizontal lockup |
| **Secondary Logo** | Full color on dark background | Horizontal lockup |
| **Icon/Symbol Only** | App icon, favicon, social media | Square format |
| **Monochrome** | Black, white, single color | All formats |
| **Wordmark Only** | When symbol isn't needed | Horizontal |

### Technical Specs
- **File Formats**: 
  - Vector: `.svg`, `.eps`, `.ai` (for print/web)
  - Raster: `.png` with transparency (multiple sizes: 512px, 256px, 128px, 64px, 32px)
  - Favicon: `.ico` (16x16, 32x32)
- **Scalability**: Must work from 16px favicon to billboard size
- **Clear Space**: Minimum clear space around logo = height of "E" in ETAnalytics
- **Minimum Size**: Logo legible at 24px height (for digital), 0.5" (for print)

## 9. What to Avoid

### ❌ Don't Do This
- Generic stock imagery (globe, handshake, upward arrow)
- Overly complex designs that don't scale
- Trendy gradients that will date quickly
- Clipart-style illustrations
- Multiple colors (keep it 2-3 max)
- Overly literal interpretations (e.g., an actual register book)
- Silicon Valley "startup" aesthetic (we're B2B enterprise)

### ✅ Do This Instead
- Clean, modern, memorable
- Scalable and versatile
- Timeless design that won't need redesign in 3 years
- Professional but distinctive
- Works in monochrome (for various applications)

## 13. Key Questions for Designer

1. **Symbol vs Wordmark**: Do you recommend a symbol + wordmark, or wordmark-only?
2. **Name Format**: "ETAnalytics" (one word) vs "ET Analytics" (two words)?
3. **European Appeal**: How do we ensure it resonates with European financial culture (not too Silicon Valley)?

## 14. Success Criteria

The logo will be successful if it:

✅ **Conveys trust and intelligence** at first glance  
✅ **Scales perfectly** from 16px favicon to billboard  
✅ **Differentiates** us from generic financial software  
✅ **Resonates** with European ETF issuer executives  
✅ **Works in all contexts** (light/dark bg, color/monochrome)  
✅ **Feels modern** but not trendy (should age well for 10+ years)  
✅ **Is memorable** (someone could describe it to a colleague)
