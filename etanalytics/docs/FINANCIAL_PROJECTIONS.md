## Executive Summary

ETAnalytics provides shareholder register analytics and investor identification services to ETF issuers in the European UCITS market. This document presents financial projections based on the total addressable market (TAM), serviceable addressable market (SAM), and realistic penetration scenarios over a 5-year horizon.

**Key Highlights:**
- **TAM**: €2.57 trillion AUM across ~120+ ETF issuers
- **Target**: €8.5M ARR by Year 5 (Conservative) to €26M ARR (Aggressive)
- **Breakeven**: Year 2 (Base Case)
- **Potential Customers**: 40-80 ETF issuers across Europe

### 3.2 Total Addressable Market (TAM)

```
TAM = All European UCITS ETF Issuers × Average Contract Value

Conservative: 120 issuers × £120,000 avg = £14.4M
Base Case: 120 issuers × £150,000 avg = £18.0M  
Optimistic: 150 issuers × £180,000 avg = £27.0M
```

### 3.3 Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)

Focusing on Ireland-domiciled issuers (largest UCITS hub) and issuers with >€1bn AUM:

```
SAM = Irish-domiciled + UK-focused issuers with disclosure needs

Target: ~80 issuers × £140,000 avg = £11.2M
```

### 3.4 Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)

Realistic Year 5 penetration of 30-50% of SAM:

```
SOM (Year 5): 25-40 customers × £150,000 avg = £3.75M - £6.0M
```
