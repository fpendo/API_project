### 2.2 Irish Companies Act 2014 - The Legal Moat

**Section 1062** grants Irish-domiciled companies the statutory right to request beneficial ownership information from shareholders. This is ETAnalytics' unique competitive advantage:

- **Legal Compulsion:** Custodians must respond within 14 days
- **No US Equivalent:** 13F filings only show quarterly snapshots, not real-time ownership
- **Operationalization Gap:** No platform currently automates this process at scale

## 3. The Problem: The €3 Trillion Blind Spot

### 3.1 Why Issuers Can't See Their Investors

European ETF issuers face a fundamental transparency problem:

```
INVESTOR JOURNEY (Hidden from Issuer):

Retail Investor → Neo-Broker → Custodian → CSD → Nominee → Share Register
                     ↓
                 INVISIBLE
                     ↓
Issuer sees: "CREST DEPOSITORY NOMINEES LIMITED" (not the actual investor)
```

**The Custody Chain Problem:**
- Average custody chain: **3-5 layers deep**
- Share registers show nominees, not beneficial owners
- 70% of trades occur off-exchange (no consolidated tape)
- Issuers have **5-15% visibility** into actual ownership

### 3.2 Business Impact of the Blind Spot

| **Board Reporting Gaps** | Can't answer "who owns our funds?" | Data-driven investor intelligence |
