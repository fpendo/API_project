## Critical Provisions

### 1. Agency Authority (Clause 4)
- ETAnalytics acts as **Client's authorized agent** for sending Disclosure Requests under Irish Companies Act 2014
- Client must provide **Authorization Letter** (Schedule 4)
- Client **indemnifies** ETAnalytics for acting within scope of authority

### 2. Data Protection (Clause 5)
- Client = **Data Controller** for Share Register Data
- ETAnalytics = **Data Processor** 
- Full **GDPR compliance** via Data Processing Addendum (Schedule 5)
- ETAnalytics will **NOT share** Client data with third parties (except for disclosure requests)

### 3. Intellectual Property (Clause 6)
- Client **owns** Share Register Data and Output Reports
- ETAnalytics **owns** Platform, Entity Database, and algorithms
- No reverse engineering permitted

### 4. Liability Cap (Clause 10)
- **Starter/Professional:** 100% of annual fees
- **Enterprise:** 150% of annual fees
- **Data breach (gross negligence):** 200% of annual fees
- **Excluded:** Consequential loss, loss of profits

### 5. Termination (Clause 11)
- For cause: 30 days cure period
- For convenience: 90 days notice
- On termination: Return/delete data, final report export

## Negotiation Points

⚠️ **Clauses likely to be negotiated by sophisticated clients:**

1. **Liability cap** - Enterprise clients may push for higher caps
2. **SLA credits** - May want credits at Professional tier
3. **Termination notice** - May want shorter notice periods
4. **Audit rights** - May want more frequent or extensive audit rights
5. **Data retention** - May want shorter retention post-termination
6. **Exclusivity** - Some clients may want exclusivity in their product category (resist this)
7. **Insurance requirements** - May want specified coverage levels
