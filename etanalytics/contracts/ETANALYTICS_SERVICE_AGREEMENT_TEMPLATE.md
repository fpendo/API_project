## BACKGROUND

(A) ETAnalytics provides beneficial ownership analytics services to ETF issuers, enabling them to identify the investment decision makers behind nominee accounts on their share registers.

(B) The Client is an ETF issuer with Irish-domiciled UCITS ETF products and wishes to engage ETAnalytics to provide beneficial ownership identification and reporting services.

## 2. SERVICES

### 2.1 Scope of Services

Subject to the terms of this Agreement, ETAnalytics shall provide the following Services to the Client:

(a) **Share Register Analysis**: Ingestion, validation, and analysis of the Client's Share Register Data;

(b) **Entity Matching**: Automated matching of nominee account names against the Entity Database to identify known entities including central securities depositories, global custodians, wealth managers, private banks, family offices, and other intermediaries;

(c) **Disclosure Workflow**: Generation and dispatch of Disclosure Requests on behalf of the Client, tracking of responses, follow-up on non-compliant parties, and reconciliation of multi-layer custody chains;

(d) **Ownership Intelligence**: Identification of Investment Decision Makers, production of Output Reports, and provision of dashboard access through the Platform;

(e) **Change Tracking**: Monitoring of changes in entity holdings for matched entities and provision of alerts as specified in the Service Level Agreement;

(f) **Support**: Technical and operational support as specified in the Service Level Agreement.

### 2.2 Service Tier

The specific service levels, frequency of analysis, number of ETF Products covered, and support response times shall be as set out in Schedule 3 based on the service tier selected by the Client (Starter, Professional, or Enterprise).

### 2.3 Service Delivery

ETAnalytics shall:

(a) perform the Services with reasonable skill, care, and diligence;

(b) use commercially reasonable efforts to meet the service levels set out in the Service Level Agreement;

(c) comply with all applicable laws and regulations in the performance of the Services;

(d) maintain appropriate personnel, facilities, and systems to perform the Services;

(e) provide the Client with reasonable notice of any planned maintenance or service interruptions.

### 2.4 Limitations

(a) ETAnalytics does not guarantee that Disclosure Requests will receive responses from all custodians or intermediaries. While Irish-domiciled companies have statutory rights to request disclosure under the Irish Companies Act 2014, compliance by third parties is outside ETAnalytics' control.

(b) Entity matching accuracy is estimated at 95% or higher but is not guaranteed. ETAnalytics shall use commercially reasonable efforts to maintain and improve the accuracy of the Entity Database.

(c) The Services are designed to identify Investment Decision Makers and do not extend to identifying individual retail investors.

# SCHEDULE 5: DATA PROCESSING ADDENDUM

## 1. Scope

This Data Processing Addendum ("DPA") forms part of the Service Agreement and applies to the processing of Personal Data by ETAnalytics on behalf of the Client.

## 2. Definitions

In this DPA, "Personal Data", "Data Controller", "Data Processor", "Data Subject", "Processing", and "Personal Data Breach" have the meanings given in UK GDPR.

## 3. Processing Details

| Item | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Subject Matter** | Processing of Personal Data contained in Share Register Data for the purpose of providing beneficial ownership analytics services |
| **Duration** | Duration of the Service Agreement plus retention period in Clause 5.5 |
| **Nature and Purpose** | Analysis, matching, storage, and reporting of share ownership data; sending Disclosure Requests to custodians |
| **Types of Personal Data** | Names of nominees and account holders; account identifiers; shareholding quantities |
| **Categories of Data Subjects** | Nominees, custodians, and other entities appearing on share registers |

## 4. Client's Obligations (as Data Controller)

The Client shall:

(a) ensure it has a lawful basis for providing Personal Data to ETAnalytics;

(b) ensure Data Subjects have been informed of the processing;

(c) respond to Data Subject requests and inform ETAnalytics of any requests received that relate to ETAnalytics' processing;

(d) ensure instructions to ETAnalytics comply with applicable data protection laws.

## 5. ETAnalytics' Obligations (as Data Processor)

ETAnalytics shall:

(a) process Personal Data only on documented instructions from the Client;

(b) ensure personnel are bound by confidentiality obligations;

(c) implement appropriate security measures (see Section 6);

(d) not engage sub-processors without prior written consent;

(e) assist the Client with Data Subject requests;

(f) assist the Client with data protection impact assessments and prior consultations;

(g) notify the Client within 24 hours of becoming aware of a Personal Data Breach;

(h) delete or return Personal Data upon termination (at Client's choice);

(i) provide information and allow audits to demonstrate compliance.

## 6. Security Measures

ETAnalytics implements the following security measures:

**Technical Measures:**
- Encryption of data at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+)
- Access controls with role-based permissions
- Multi-factor authentication for all personnel
- Regular security patching and vulnerability assessments
- Intrusion detection and prevention systems
- Regular backups with encryption

**Organisational Measures:**
- Information security policy
- Staff training on data protection
- Background checks for personnel with data access
- Incident response procedures
- Regular security reviews

## 7. Sub-processors

ETAnalytics may engage the following categories of sub-processors:

| Category | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| Cloud hosting providers | Data storage and Platform hosting |
| Email service providers | Sending Disclosure Requests |
| Analytics tools | Platform performance monitoring |

The Client consents to the engagement of sub-processors in these categories, provided ETAnalytics ensures sub-processors are bound by equivalent data protection obligations.

## 8. International Transfers

ETAnalytics shall not transfer Personal Data outside the UK or EEA without appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses or adequacy decision).

## 9. Data Subject Rights

ETAnalytics shall assist the Client in responding to requests from Data Subjects to exercise their rights under UK GDPR, including: access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection.

## 10. Personal Data Breach

In the event of a Personal Data Breach, ETAnalytics shall:

(a) notify the Client within 24 hours of becoming aware;

(b) provide details of the breach, affected data, and remedial actions;

(c) cooperate with the Client's investigation and notification obligations;

(d) take reasonable steps to mitigate the effects of the breach.
