# Knowledge Vault & Research Swarm

Part of [[Home]]. See [[Overview]].

A **self-directed research agent swarm** builds the base knowledge layer in the Obsidian vault, running intensively for **4–8 weeks**, then topping up forever.

## The 6-stage loop

1. **Planner sets the questions** — turns the goal ("understand beef farming deeply for this farm") into a prioritised tree of open questions (by impact on profit, welfare, cost); re-plans each cycle.
2. **Dispatch the agent swarm** — spawns specialist agents in parallel, one per domain; each runs its own search → read → extract loop with source-cited findings.
3. **Specialists go deep** — each reads the literature, extracts structured claims with citations, and generates its own follow-up questions (depth compounds); conflicting evidence is flagged, not hidden.
4. **Synthesise & deduplicate** — merges overlapping findings, reconciles contradictions, writes atomic cross-linked notes.
5. **Write to the Obsidian vault** — synthesised notes become the permanent base intelligence layer (versioned, backed up).
6. **Gap & quality review → loop** — scores coverage, surfaces gaps, routes new questions back to the planner (broaden) or the swarm (go deeper). The loop never truly stops.

## The 6 domain agents

| Agent | Focus |
|-------|-------|
| **Genetics** | Breed comparison, genomic selection, EBVs, fertility & calving ease |
| **Soil & agronomy** | pH, nutrients, organic matter, sward composition, reseeding |
| **Weather & climate** | Growing-degree days, drought/frost risk, grass-growth windows |
| **Animal health** | Parasites, vaccination, lameness, mineral status, biosecurity |
| **Nutrition & forage** | Silage quality, ration balancing, energy/protein, finishing diets |
| **Economics & market** | Deadweight pricing, input costs, subsidies, margin per head |

Each domain has ~8 sub-topics (e.g. Genetics → EBVs, genomic selection, maternal traits, feed conversion, marbling, heat/disease tolerance, crossbreeding, calving ease).

## Vault categories (what ends up stored)

Live Weather Readings · Soil & Field Data · Cattle Records · Feed & Nutrition · Health & Biosecurity · **Research Vault** (2,000+ papers, structured & source-cited) · Financial & Compliance.

The research vault holds peer-reviewed papers, AHDB trial data, breed-society EBV datasets, vet clinical protocols, soil science, agronomy, carbon/environmental data, and market economics.
