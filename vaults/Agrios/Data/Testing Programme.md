# Testing Programme (Full Profile)

Part of [[Home]]. Sized for a **50-head herd** on 500 acres. Lab results land by email and are auto-ingested into the vault.

## Summary of the five test streams

| Stream | Cadence | Samples/yr | Cost | Notes |
|--------|---------|-----------|------|-------|
| **Soil** | 4-yr rotation, 1 quadrant/yr | ~10 composites | ~£700/yr | Each composite = 20–25 bulked cores |
| **Silage & Hay (forage)** | Every new lot + winter spot checks | ~7 | ~£280/yr | Mycotoxin panel risk-triggered only |
| **Genetics** | One-off per animal | 50 (one-off) | **£3,500 one-off** | Not recurring — sequenced once |
| **Water** | Biannual troughs + annual borehole | ~10 | ~£350/yr | Microbiology + minerals |
| **Animal health** | FEC + blood profiles on subsets | ~50 tests | ~£900/yr | Not every animal every time |

> Key correction made during the build: **genetics is a one-off cost**, not annual. Sample sizes reflect **50 cattle** (down from 250). Earlier "£21K/yr" figure was over-stated and removed.

## Soil (detail)

500 acres → 4 quadrants (~125 ac) → 4 cells each (~12.5 ha). AHDB recommends routine chemistry every 3–4 years per field, so one quadrant (4 cells) is fully sampled each year (every cell once per 4 years). Each cell yields 2–3 geo-tagged composites (~10/yr total). Analytes: pH, P, K, Mg, S, organic matter, CEC, bulk density/compaction. Labs: NRM / Eurofins Agro.

## Genetics (two-tier)

- **Tier 1 — Elite full genome (30× WGS):** bulls + high-value breeding females (~5 animals × £150)
- **Tier 2 — Light 50K SNP panel:** commercial herd (~45 animals × £60)
- Yields EBVs, breed composition, parentage confirmation, recessive-condition screening. Stored permanently. Labs: Weatherbys / Neogen.

## Forage, Water, Animal health

- **Forage:** DM, ME, crude protein, D-value, NDF/ADF, fermentation pH, ammonia-N. Labs: Trouw Nutrition / Sciantec.
- **Water:** coliforms/E. coli, nitrate, iron, sulphate, hardness, TDS. Labs: ALS / SWS.
- **Animal health:** faecal egg counts (10 animals × 3 rounds), trace-element blood (8 × 2/yr), disease screen BVD/IBR/Johne's (6 animals). Labs: Exmoor Vets / APHA.

Each test stream has a **5-step lab workflow** (sample → send → lab → results by email → auto-ingest to vault) and a card showing latest analyte values with good/watch/act status.
