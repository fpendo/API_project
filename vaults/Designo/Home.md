# Designo Studio

**AI-powered web design agency in a box.** Designo generates cinematic, motion-driven websites from a questionnaire and photos — then goes further: it finds UK businesses that have no website, builds them a speculative mockup, pitches them by email, takes payment via Stripe, and delivers a weekly SEO + ASO report. One operator (Angie Pendarves) runs the whole agency from a single portal.

- **Live at:** `https://<server>/designo/` (public landing — itself generated by Designo's own pipeline)
- **Codebase:** `/opt/app/designo/` (backend + frontend + storage)
- **Backend service:** `designo-backend` (systemd, FastAPI on `127.0.0.1:8620`)
- **Model:** `claude-fable-5` (two-stage: Creative Director → Motion Website Builder)

## One-line summary

Scrape businesses with no website → Fable writes their brief and builds a cinematic mockup with AI artwork → personalised pitch email with inline scrolling GIF and private login → prospect views their site, reads the proposal, pays £695 by card → welcome pack fires, domain registered, site goes live → £59/month covers hosting, updates and a Monday SEO + ASO report.

## Key facts

- **Pricing:** £695 one-off build · £59/month ongoing (both configurable; domain year 1 in the £695, renewals in the £59)
- **Founder persona:** Angie Pendarves — 15 years at Cheil (Samsung's agency), built Samsung's concierge app, ran Colombia's first digital election campaign (Santos)
- **USP:** every site ships a machine-readable **shadow site** (`llms.txt`, `agent.json`, `agent.html`, JSON-LD) — built for AI agents, not just Google
- **Report:** weekly **SEO + ASO** — Google Search Console data plus AI-crawler visits, AI-assistant referrals, and shadow-site health
