# Agent Economy Positioning

Part of [[Home]]. See [[SEO + ASO Report]], [[Client Journey]].

## The pitch

"Beautiful websites, built for the agent economy." Public-facing copy never mentions the systematic pipeline — just that Designo builds cinematic sites that are legible to both humans **and** the AI assistants increasingly doing the searching and buying.

## The shadow site (the USP)

Every Designo site ships a machine-readable layer alongside the human one:

- `llms.txt` — plain-language summary for language models
- `agent.json` — structured business data (services, hours, contact, pricing)
- `agent.html` — semantic, chrome-free HTML
- **Schema.org JSON-LD** injected into the `<head>`

This *is* agent search optimisation. When an AI assistant answers "find me a roofer in Truro", the shadow site is what makes the client findable and quotable.

## The numbers (researched 2025–26, cited in proposal + landing)

- ChatGPT: **800M weekly users**, ~2bn questions a day
- ~**50% of Google searches** now open with an AI answer, reaching 2bn+ people/month
- Organic clicks fall **~60%** when an AI answer appears; **68%** of searches end with no click (Seer/SparkToro)
- AI-referred traffic grew **~1,200%** in a year and converts at **2.5×** Google organic (Chartbeat/Similarweb)

Presented as the "Why now — the numbers" stat cards in the proposal (with a "what this means for you" note tying it to the shadow site) and as the cinematic "THE SHIFT" count-up section on the landing page.
