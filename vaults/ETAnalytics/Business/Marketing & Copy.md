# Marketing & Copy

Part of [[Home]].

## LANDING_PAGE_COPY_V2.md

Optimized B2B copy targeting the **Head of Capital Markets / Distribution** at ETF issuers.

- Hero: *"Know Your Investors. Grow Your Assets."*
- Problem framed at board level: *"We have £5 billion in AUM. We have no idea who owns it."*
- Stats as narratives: *"$3.1T → Zero visibility"*
- Solution emphasizes **operationalizing Irish statutory disclosure rights**
- Features as outcomes: *"Know Immediately What You Own"*, *"We Handle the Paperwork"*
- Documented expected lifts: +15–25% CTA, +20% problem engagement

## LANDING_PAGE_IMPROVEMENT_PROMPT.md

Research brief for AI copywriting:
- **$3T blind spot** — custody chains 3–5 layers deep
- **Business impact** — lost revenue, blind distribution, regulatory risk
- **Unique legal advantage** — Irish Companies Act disclosure powers (78% of European ETFs)
- **Persona** — age 38–55, £150K–£400K comp, KPIs = AUM growth / net flows
- Copy principles: PAS framework, specificity, loss aversion, social proof
