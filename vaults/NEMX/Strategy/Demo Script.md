# Demo Script (Natural England)

Part of [[Home]]. Source: `/opt/app/DEMO_SCRIPT.md`. Structured 5-part narrative. See [[Business Flows]].

1. **Scheme submission & approval** — `/submission-portal` → `/regulator`
2. **Credit generation** — redeem 50 tonnes → 5,000,000 credits at `/landowner`
3. **Exchange trading** — developer browses/buys at `/developer` → Exchange tab
4. **Planning QR flow** — developer generates QR (auto scheme selection + on-chain lock) → officer validates at `/planning` → approve (burn) or reject (unlock)
5. **System-wide** — regulator archive, operator OTC desk

**Closing benefits:** transparency, accountability, efficiency, regulatory oversight, user-friendly QR workflow.
