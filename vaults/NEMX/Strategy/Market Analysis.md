# Market Analysis — UK Nutrient Neutrality

Part of [[Home]]. Source: `/opt/app/MARKET_ANALYSIS_UK_NUTRIENT_NEUTRALITY.md`.

## TAM

**£400M–£1.1B annually.**

## Key metrics

- 27 river catchments, 74 LPAs (140+ by 2030)
- ~120,000 homes delayed; 16,500 homes/year affected (8% of supply)
- £140M government investment in mitigation schemes
- Credit pricing: **£2,500–£7,000/dwelling** (nitrogen); up to **£100,000/kg** (phosphorus in constrained catchments)
- Platform fee capture at 3–5%: **£1.7M–£14.4M/year** depending on scenario

## Priority catchments

- **Solent** — mature
- **Somerset** — emerging
- **Tees** — government-backed

## Regulatory outlook

Planning and Infrastructure Act 2025 → Environmental Delivery Plans (EDPs), Nature Restoration Fund, WWTW upgrade deadline **April 2030**.
