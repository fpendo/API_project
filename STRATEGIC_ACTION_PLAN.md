# STRATEGIC ACTION PLAN
## UK Nutrient Neutrality Blockchain Registry Platform

**Period:** 2026-2028 (24 Months)  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** January 2026

## EXECUTIVE OVERVIEW

This action plan outlines the phased approach to launching and scaling a blockchain-based nutrient neutrality registry platform in the UK market. The plan is structured around three phases: Foundation (Months 1-6), Pilot Launch (Months 7-12), and Market Expansion (Months 13-24).
